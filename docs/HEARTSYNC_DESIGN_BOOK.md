# 1. Vision

HeartSync is not just another couples application.

It is a living digital world where two people build, protect and grow their relationship together.

The application should feel magical, emotional and peaceful rather than social-media-like.

The world itself should react to the relationship.

The relationship becomes alive.

---

# 2. Core Philosophy

The application must never encourage addiction.

The application must never replace the real relationship.

Its purpose is to strengthen communication,
create memories,
increase empathy,
and motivate couples to spend quality time together.

HeartSync is a companion.

Not a replacement.
